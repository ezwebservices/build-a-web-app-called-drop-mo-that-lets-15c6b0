# Drop — Marketing Email Sequence

For people who signed up on the waitlist or landing page but haven't started a drop yet. Voice: group chat energy, not newsletter energy. Short. No corporate sympathy.

---

## Email 1 — Welcome (sent immediately)

**Subject:** you're in 💧
**Preview:** here's what Drop actually is, in 90 seconds

Hey —

You're on the list for Drop.

Quick thing so you know what you signed up for:

Drop is for the moment someone you love is going through it — flood, hospital bill, layoff, whatever — and you want to rally a bunch of people to help **without** the recipient ever knowing it was organized.

You start a private drop. You invite the crew by email. Everyone pledges in secret. On drop day, everyone hits send on Venmo at the same moment. Their phone lights up. They have no idea it wasn't spontaneous.

Whenever the moment hits, we'll be here.

## Email 2 — Day 2 — How it actually works

**Subject:** the part most people don't expect
**Preview:** the recipient is never a user. ever.

A thing we get asked a lot:

> "Does the recipient sign up?"

No. The recipient is never a user of Drop. No account, no email from us, no notification, nothing.

They just experience their phone lighting up with Venmos from everyone who loves them, in roughly the same 60 seconds, on a day they were not expecting it.

That's the whole product.

We thought about adding a "thank you" page for the recipient. We killed it. The surprise is sacred.
