How it works

Three steps. Zero suspicion.

It's a group chat that turns into a wave of Venmos hitting their phone at the same moment. No donation page. No public shame. Just friends pulling something off together.

  1. STEP 01

    Start a drop

    Add their first name, their Venmo handle, a short story, and a drop day. That's the whole setup.

  2. STEP 02

    Rally the crew

    Paste in emails. We send a branded invite with a live progress image. Friends pledge in 30 seconds — no account required.

  3. DROP DAY

    The wave lands

    At the scheduled minute, everyone gets a pre-filled Venmo link. They tap send. The recipient's phone erupts.

Questions the crew asks

Does Drop handle the money? +

No. Money moves directly friend-to-recipient via Venmo. Drop coordinates the timing and delivers a pre-filled deep link so it happens in sync.

Will the recipient find out? +

Not from us. Drop never emails them, never lists the page publicly, and uses unguessable links. Only invitees can see the drop.

Do contributors need an account? +

Nope. They click the invite, pledge in 30 seconds, and get the Venmo link on drop day. Only organizers sign in.

What if someone can't send on drop day? +

Their pledge waits. They can open the email later and still send with one tap — the wave just has a few stragglers, which is fine.

Can I postpone the drop? +

Yes. Edit the drop day any time before it fires and everyone is automatically re-notified.
