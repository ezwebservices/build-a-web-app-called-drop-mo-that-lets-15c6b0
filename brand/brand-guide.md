# Drop — Brand Guide

## Name
**Drop** (product: *Drop-mo*) — short, punchy, verb-ready. "Start a drop." "Join the drop." "Drop day."

## Tagline
**Rally the group. Surprise the one.**

Alternates:
- *Pull off the surprise.*
- *They feel the love. They never see it coming.*

## One-line pitch
Drop is the easiest way to coordinate a surprise Venmo flood for a friend or family member going through it — everyone pledges in private, then sends at the same moment on drop day.

## Origin
A friend's basement flooded. The fix isn't a charity page — it's a group chat that turns into a wave of Venmos hitting their phone all at once. The surprise IS the product.

## Audience
- **Organizers** (primary): 25–45, lives in group texts, organizes the birthday dinner, has Venmo, has empathy, has zero patience for clunky UX.
- **Contributors**: friends-of-organizer. Will pledge in 30 seconds on a phone or not at all.
- **Recipient**: never sees Drop. Just sees their phone light up.

## Voice
**Hype but considerate. Conspiratorial-in-a-good-way.**

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Sound like a group chat planning a surprise party | Sound like a 501(c)(3) fundraising platform |
| "Let's pull this off." | "Thank you for your generous donation." |
| Use the verb *drop* freely | Use the word *donor* or *campaign* in user-facing copy |
| Short sentences. Real warmth. | Corporate sympathy. Stock-photo empathy. |
| Treat secrecy as sacred — it's the whole point | Mention the recipient by full name anywhere |

### Word list
- **Yes:** drop, rally, pledge, the crew, the surprise, drop day, pull it off, the wave
- **No:** donor, donation, campaign, beneficiary, fundraiser, GoFundMe-style anything

## Visual identity
- **Logo mark:** a downward water-drop silhouette with a subtle upward arrow inside — money "dropping in," love "rising up."
- **Primary color:** *Splash* — a confident, joyful teal-cyan. Reads as water, generosity, and a Venmo-adjacent blue without copying it.
- **Accent:** *Surprise* — warm coral, used sparingly for the moment of impact (drop-day buttons, success states).
- **Type:** Inter (UI) + a slightly playful display weight for hero moments. Tight tracking, generous line-height.
- **Motion:** Framer Motion. Things *land*. Counter ticks up with a soft bounce. Pledges arrive with a subtle ripple.

## The feeling
Opening a Drop should feel like being let in on a secret with people who love the same person you do.
